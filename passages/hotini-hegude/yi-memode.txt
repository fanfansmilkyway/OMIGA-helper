    										yi.  memode
    metsuhoku, yisikarv ing 'weki' hotini. kimo oto yahuku memode.
    sowe, metsu desu ing yi mokuro nido sihodo. sore mokuronido sihorv desu waro aki, kimo hikima iko pvsv we yotai. sikasi, metsu ponite ing sihodo. sihorv kio waro koang, so kimo hikima metsu a tabu metsu du ni. metsu du huki noe kare engtou, a gotv hotini to sito sihorv.
    huki gotode hotini, dv ing 'weki' hotini tsu hegurv osa tvku temo:"kare engtou noku pani wa sihorv, sikasi teri du kiponi." temo du osatvsi momo desu yo kare. metsu kio de mokare sihorv desu waro hipanira!
    kue iko engtou. metsu du kosv legemu sihorv a sihoma sihorv. yitimai, imake yi hotasa -- dv seo <<u du sihorv>> tsu eisai. ai kimo hitato kare sihorv (kimo kue hikima iko sikarv we yotai), si metsu kamisa:"zazusing (kosv legemu sihorv) seng du ni desu yo nonita o yiru. a kimo du haso desu yo ruhu du nu." zazu seng noe kare, oyaho metsu humu 40 niti. momora miyode desu:"ri sada siba u. sikasi, metsu yohe de kimo nuku samuyi to sibado, kimo hikima sikarv, airu to teu sada siba kima?!
    deimasu iko engtou desu, mokue. zong sukaipo, waya kongmi hatasa, koang tsu sihorv, hiusika a gedasu tsu LURO, kare me metsu kio eike, koang, emua! kimo rato kiyani nowe hotini du hagatvsi.
    yi iko sikarv, kiuzeng, oto yira kiode a yohede zeng metsu. temo kika desu yoka. mokue komi iko sikarv. zong sukaipo, losowe -- dv oto taho yohede tsu pvsv, tangboku -- yigohe a pateni yiponi, angde kongmi o iko pvsv.
